Pivitt / About

A consultancy for brands that outgrew their own.

Pivitt is a brand transformation consultancy. We rebuild the commercial system underneath a brand so it can carry the business it has become, then stay to run it or hand it over.

Most of the businesses we work with did not have a taste problem. They had outscaled a brand built for an earlier version of themselves, and it had drifted out of step with how far they had come. The decks were all over the place, the market read them as smaller than they had become, and every attempt to fix it stalled under too many opinions.

We treat brand as a commercial system, not decoration. Strategy and execution under one partner. Every decision is tied to a business outcome and justified by evidence, not creative preference. We build the system, install the playbook, and either hand it to your team or run it for you.
