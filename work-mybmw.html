My BMW App

Awareness campaign · Automotive

A cross-channel campaign to put the My BMW App in front of owners, led by a film and carried through email, web banners and in-dealer cards.

The brief

Put the app in front of owners.

The My BMW App is where ownership lives now: navigation, vehicle health, charging and live status, all in one place. The job was to drive awareness and downloads across the channels owners and prospects actually see, from the inbox to the dealership.

The film

The app, in ninety seconds.

The campaign led with a film. It opens on the drive and moves through the app, navigation, vehicle health and staying connected, so the value is clear before anyone reaches a download button.

The campaign film, with captions.

In the inbox

Straight to the inbox.

Email signatures turned every message from BMW and MINI into a prompt to download, with a clear call to action and a look that still felt like the marque.

Email signatures for BMW and MINI.

Across the web

Banners that travel.

A set of web banners carried the same message at every size, in a dark treatment and a lighter alternative, so the campaign held wherever it ran.

Web banners, dark and light, across sizes.

In hand

A code worth scanning.

In-dealer and print cards put a QR code in reach, turning a moment on the forecourt into an install.

App cards with a scannable code.

The outcome

One app, every touchpoint.

The My BMW App campaign gave BMW and MINI a single, consistent push across film, email, web and print, all pointing to the same place: the app.
