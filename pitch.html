Pivitt / When the business outgrows the brand

Most companies outgrow their brand before they notice .

01We build and run the brand operating system behind ambitious organisations.

Strategy, positioning, identity and the assets to carry it, held together as one system rather than a set of disconnected projects. The point is not a nicer logo. It is a brand that matches the business and pulls it forward.

Fifteen years of it, for BMW Group, Universal Music Group, Rolls-Royce, the BBC, and names breaking into elite sport for the first time.

02Different problems, one through line.

Three premium brands, one flagship address, over 200,000 assets.

A rebrand that drove five elite partnerships that did not exist before.

Signal over noise. The double O became a symbol that works at any scale, from a favicon to a building.

Campaign production and optimisation rebuilt across labels and releases.

03Diagnose. Transform. Sustain.

A clear path from where the brand is to a system that keeps pace with the business. Most relationships start small, with the diagnostic, and grow from there.

A fixed, paid first step that reads where the brand stands against the business behind it, and produces a Growth Readiness Score you can act on.

Strategy, positioning, identity and the assets to carry it, built on the Pivitt 360 model across its six pillars. The work in this page is the output of this stage.

An ongoing partnership that runs and improves the brand operating system as the business keeps moving, so the brand never falls behind again.
