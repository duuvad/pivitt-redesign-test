The Pivitt 360 Model.

Six connected pillars that decide whether a brand commands attention, justifies its price and accelerates growth. We assess all six, then rebuild the ones holding the business back.

Proprietary frameworkThe 360 wheel

Six pillars, the components beneath each, across three stages. Hover a pillar to see what it holds. We assess all six, then rebuild the ones holding the business back.

01

Brand Strategy

Purpose, vision, mission and values. The internal compass every other decision answers to.

02

Target Audience

Who you are for, what they need, and how they decide. The buyers the brand has to move.

03

Unique Selling Proposition

The core difference and the customer benefit. Why you, stated so a buyer can repeat it.

04

Brand Positioning

Market context and competitive position. The space you own and defend.

05

Brand Identity

Visual and verbal expression. The evidence of the thinking, not decoration.

06

Brand Assets

Guidelines, campaigns, templates and digital materials. The system, made usable.
