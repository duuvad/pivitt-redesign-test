06London brand consultancy: the questions we get asked.

A London brand consultancy advises founders and marketing leaders on commercial positioning, brand strategy, identity and messaging. It differs from a branding agency by leading with commercial diagnosis and positioning before any creative work. Pivitt is a London-based consultancy building and improving brand operating systems for ambitious organisations.

London brand consultancy fees typically range from £1,500 for a brand diagnostic to £35,000 to £120,000 for a full brand transformation programme. Pivitt offers a £1,500 Brand Alignment Diagnostic as the entry point. Full transformation programmes covering strategy, positioning, identity, messaging and rollout run £35,000 to £120,000 depending on scope.

A brand consultancy leads with commercial diagnosis, positioning and strategy. A branding agency typically leads with creative exploration and visual identity. Consultancies charge for thinking; agencies charge for execution. Consultancies tend to work senior-led with no account management layer. Agencies usually route work through account managers to junior creative teams. Read our full comparison at /compare .

Look at four things. The seniority of the person who actually does your work, not the person who pitches. The commercial rigour of their diagnostic process. The calibre of named clients they have delivered measurable outcomes for. Whether their case studies lead with business results rather than creative deliverables. If three of those four are missing, keep looking.
